About Me

Hello, my name is Sheethal Sankar. I hail from the picturesque Palakkad district in Kerala. I come from an academically inclined family; my father and mother are both dedicated teachers, and my brother is currently pursuing his engineering studies.

I recently completed my B.Tech and have supplemented my education with a specialized course in embedded systems and C programming. My academic journey has been marked by a keen interest in technology and innovation, which has driven me to undertake several exciting projects. Notable among these are digital LSB steganography using C programming, an inverted search application utilizing C and data structures, a car black box system developed with embedded C, and an MP3 reader/editor also using C.

Currently, I am expanding my skill set by delving into web development. This new area of study excites me as I envision it to be a crucial element in my future career.

In my free time, I enjoy exploring new technologies and staying updated with the latest trends in the tech world. Apart from my technical interests, I have a passion for reading and enjoy immersing myself in books that span a variety of genres.

I am driven by a strong set of values, including dedication, continuous learning, and innovation. These principles guide my professional and personal life, helping me strive for excellence in everything I do.

Looking ahead, I am eager to apply my skills in a dynamic and challenging environment where I can contribute to impactful projects and continue to grow as a technology professional. My long-term goal is to make significant contributions to the field of technology, leveraging my knowledge and creativity to solve real-world problems.

I am excited about the future and look forward to the opportunities and challenges that lie ahead. Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me!
